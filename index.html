About Us

The CS department of Hansraj college is deemed to be one of the most eminent and engaging undergraduate departments in the entirety of Delhi University. Ordinateur, thus has had a powerful reputation of being a department comprising of extraordinary minds and is recognised and appreciated all over the Delhi University.
The society remains active year-round and conducts frequent interactive activities like quizzes, contests, knowledgable sessions, seminars, academic workshops and other cultural activites at intra and inter collegiate levels.
Having received massive appreciation and participation from students all over Delhi, the department shall always strive to keep up with the expectations of the CS students all over Delhi and shall keep providing students with the opportunities to explore the vast limitlessness of their potential.

About Cynosure

One of the most coveted and anticipated event among CS students all across India, Cynosure is the annual tech fest organised by Ordinateur, the Computer Science society of Hansraj college.
It is a culmination of various tech as well as cultural events that take place across a time period of 2-3 days every single year and is actively participated by thousands of students each year.
Events include coding competitions, tech based quizzes, trivia and many recreational games and such. Various exciting prices and a healthy and fun interaction with other tech savvy students is what makes Cynosure one of the most convivial place to be this time around.

FAQs

  • How will the whole online fest work?
    Each event in the fest is designed to be executed, managed and completed all through the online medium. Details of the online platforms to be used for the specific events will be communicated to the participants post registration.
  • Where can i find the rules and guidelines for the events?
    The pdf containing the rules and guidelines for the registered event will be sent to you a few days before the event. Furthermore, you can also find the event description by clicking on that specific event’s poster on our website.
  • Can we participate in multiple events?
    Yes you can! The event timings are designed to encourage multiple participations. Kindly note that for the last round of Inter-Battle-View, you need to ensure by yourself that your slot does not clash with other events.
  • How do we participate in teams?
    There are some events in which you are required to register as a team. In those cases, only one member would need to fill the registration form with all the details of his team members.
  • Do I have to pay to register for the events?
    There are different kinds of events under the fest some of which are paid. Of these, Code-A-Thon, Inter-Battle-View, Quizzards of Tech, Tech Labyrinth,MCU trivia are completely free while the PUBG tournament require a small registration fee.
  • What mode of payments are available to pay the registration fees of the events?
    The modes of payment accepted are GPay, PhonePe, Paytm Wallet and Paytm UPI.
